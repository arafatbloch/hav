About Our Resort

Nestled in the serene beauty of Sasan Gir, Hotel AVATARA is a peaceful getaway designed for relaxation and comfort. Whether you're seeking a family vacation, a romantic escape, or a break from the city, our resort offers spacious rooms, modern amenities, and warm hospitality. Every detail has been crafted to give you a luxurious and memorable stay.

Meet the Owner

At the heart of Hotel AVATARA is its visionary founder, Mr. Dhaval Gandhi, whose dedication to hospitality has shaped the spirit of the resort. With years of experience and a deep love for nature and service, he built Hotel AVATARA as a place where guests feel at home while surrounded by comfort and tranquility. His hands-on involvement ensures every guest experience is warm, personal, and memorable.
