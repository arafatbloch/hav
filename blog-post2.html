New Poolside Dining Experience 🥂✨

Resort Updates · July 2025

Looking to add a splash of luxury to your next vacation or weekend getaway? 🌴 At Hotel Avatara, we’re thrilled to unveil our new Poolside Dining Experience, a beautifully curated culinary journey set beside the shimmering waters of our elegant pool. This is not just a meal – it's an experience that blends taste, ambiance, and relaxation for the ultimate resort indulgence. 🍽️💫

🌅 Dine Under the Stars, Beside the Pool: Imagine a calm evening, golden hour skies, the gentle reflection of moonlight on the pool’s surface, and a plate of gourmet cuisine in front of you. Whether you’re staying with us or visiting for a special evening, our poolside dining area is designed to deliver an unforgettable ambiance. Elegant lighting, cozy seating, and subtle music set the stage for a relaxing and romantic night.

  • 💕 Perfect for couples seeking a romantic dinner
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Ideal for family bonding and laughter
  • 🎉 Great for friend groups and celebrations
  • 🧘‍♂️ Tranquil escape for solo travelers

🍴 A Curated Menu with Local & Global Flavors: Our chefs have gone above and beyond to craft a menu that delights every palate. From regional favorites to globally inspired dishes, the poolside menu reflects both freshness and finesse.

  • 🐟 Grilled seafood platters
  • 🍕 Gourmet pizzas & flatbreads
  • 🥗 Seasonal salads and starters
  • 🌶️ Gujarati specialties with a modern twist
  • 🍫 Indulgent desserts like molten chocolate cake
  • 🍹 Handcrafted fruit mocktails & beverages

Vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options are also available, ensuring everyone can enjoy the experience fully.

🍷 Elegant Service, Elevated Experience: What sets our poolside dining apart is the attention to every detail. From the moment you're seated, our attentive staff ensures you’re pampered with warm hospitality and impeccable service. Whether it’s a birthday surprise, a candlelight setup, or a special dietary request — we’re here to make it happen.

📸 Instagram-Worthy Moments Await: With beautifully plated dishes, cozy lighting, and a picturesque pool backdrop, your dinner here is sure to become a cherished memory — and a highlight on your Instagram feed! Don’t forget to tag us: @hotelavataraofficial
